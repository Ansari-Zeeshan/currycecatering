Welcome to Curryce A Catering Service


Who we Are ? We are pleased to provide you with our corporate catering proposal to your company employees. We feel there is a need for home-made style-tasty food for employees who constantly look for healthier but tastier food options with greater choice. Our aim is to reach people who would like to tuck in some satisfying meals that provides a stronger sense of connection to safe home-made food as we all know this always beats Restaurant-style cooking.
We specialize in South-Indian meals and curries, includes both Veg and Non-veg. We have a menu that is fresh and different every day. We offer customizable meal boxes. Our meals are prepared in hygienic, spacious centralized kitchen by professional cooks from regional Andhra and Tamil Nadu to deliver authentic flavor and taste.
We have cooks of caliber who are aligned with our motive and deliver best experience through honest food, with standards which are regarded as the highest of quality. In addition to customizable meal boxes, we are flexible to offer services to companies that have their own canteens. Our caterers will cook and serve food, dished out to employees.

How Curryce Catering Services Started ?

Curryce comes from a family who is passionate and celebrates food and cooking, a long line of great home cooks that put great food on the table. We believe in authentic taste and flavor that instantly connects people to traditional recipes and home-style food. Curryce is a start-up run by Chandrakala who has 21 years of experience in Indian and Global Cuisine. She has served thousands of customers in London under the brand PaninoD'oro Deli for 13 years, starting her career in 1995 working for Burger King. Her interest in cooking was inspired by her family which relishes rich traditional recipes passed down by generations. Curryce carries the tradition and flavor that is 75 years old.

How we prepare Our food

Meal Preparation (Precaution and Measure)

We never use frozen goods or stored vegetable stored in our kitchen container and also vegetables that are preserved in freezes for a longer time.

We always use fresh vegetables and our goods are always having superior quality which are produce on daily basis, Fresh locals are also produce procured on daily basis.

Our Meats are having best quality and cut hygienically so that it doesn't get spoiled and get polluted And wash them very carefully to ensure that it doesn't contain any germs and insects, After that we use it for Cooking.

We doesn't add any additives and preservatives for our meal, to make it uncomfortable and chances of getting spoiled food to eat.

Our chef doesn't use any food colors or dyes to make it more tasty or spicey food, They always use materials which are hygienic to eat and less oily.
